What are the 6 Traits?

Ideas, Organization, Voice, Word Choice, Sentence Fluency, Conventions.

Ideas

Clear message and focused content with relevant details.

Organization

Logical structure, effective beginning, middle and end.

Voice

Writer’s personality and tone appropriate to purpose/audience.

Word Choice

Precise, vivid vocabulary that enhances meaning.

Sentence Fluency

Varied, well-crafted sentences that flow smoothly.

Conventions

Correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, capitalization.
